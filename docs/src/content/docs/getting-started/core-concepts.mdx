Before diving into ArchVault, it helps to understand the core concepts that shape how the application is organized.

## C4 Model Levels

- **System Context (L1)** — shows how your system interacts with users and external systems
- **Container (L2)** — zooms into a system to show its containers (applications, databases, etc.)
- **Component (L3)** — zooms into a container to show its internal components

## Organizations

Organizations are the top-level grouping for teams and workspaces. Each organization has members with roles.

## Workspaces

A workspace is a collection of architecture elements (systems, containers, components) and diagrams that belong to an organization.

## Diagrams

Diagrams are visual representations of your architecture at a specific C4 level, built from the elements in your workspace.
